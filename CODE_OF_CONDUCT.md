# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project a respectful, harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:

- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Respecting different viewpoints and experiences
- Giving constructive feedback
- Accepting responsibility and apologizing when needed
- Focusing on what is best for the community

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- Harassment or discriminatory language
- Personal attacks
- Trolling or insulting comments
- Publishing private information without permission
- Other conduct that could reasonably be considered inappropriate

## Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying standards of acceptable behavior and may remove, edit, or reject contributions that do not align with this Code of Conduct.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all project spaces, including issues, pull requests, discussions, documentation, and community channels.

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive or unacceptable behavior may be reported to the project maintainers. Reports will be reviewed and addressed fairly.
