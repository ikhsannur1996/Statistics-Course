# Syllabus

## Statistics Course

A complete interactive course for Statistics, Data Science, Machine Learning, and Business Intelligence.

## Target Audience

- Beginners in Statistics
- Data Analysts
- Business Intelligence Developers
- Data Scientists
- Machine Learning Engineers
- Researchers
- Master's and PhD Students

## Learning Flow

Each lesson follows this structure:

1. Introduction
2. Story
3. Visualization
4. Theory
5. Mathematics
6. Python
7. Exercise
8. Mini Project
9. Quiz
10. Summary
